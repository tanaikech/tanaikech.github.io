This was expressed by the comprehension. data = ['a', 'b', 'c', 'd', 'b', 'c', 'd', 'b', 'c', 'b'] result = sorted({i: data.count(i) for i in set(data)}.items(), key=lambda x: x[1], reverse=True) print(result) >>> [('b', 4), ('c', 3), ('d', 2), ('a', 1)]
