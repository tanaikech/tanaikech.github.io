About Me

I’m Japanese and scientist with Ph.D. in Physics. My curiosity is to quest, think and create. Especially, I always like to create new things which are not exist in this world yet.

Basically I find such new things during sleep. I had performed most of my large discoveries during sleep. In fact, I had discovered new materials, structures, methods, designs and etc during sleep so far. Although new discovery during sleep depends on my mental and physical states, I had produced various new things for the real world so far. These are published as academic papers, patents, newspapers and some products, and also have been awarded prizes. I could confirm own thinking faculty by these achievements.

I had worked at local and international research institutes and universities as both a researcher and a teacher. One day, I thought suddenly that my experiences may be able to be helpful for people and other fields. So from August 2016, I have begun to look new direction that I have never touched, while I continue own researches. Now I’m receiving new wind. This wind is sophisticating my knowledge, and it gives me the possibility of new fields. I would like to grow more this wind using my experiences. I would like to create new fields.

I launched Blog, GitHub, Gists, Google+, Qiita and Stackoverflow. Although now I’m still groping, I continue to introduce the wind here. I am glad, if those could be useful for you and/or you are interested in my life.
