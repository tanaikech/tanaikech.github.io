Profile of Tanaike - Physicist, GDE, and Developer

About Me

I am a Japanese scientist holding a Ph.D. in Physics and a Google Developer Expert (GDE) for Google Workspace. Recently, I was awarded first place in the Outstanding GDE Award on September 26, 2025.

I am driven by a deep curiosity to explore the frontiers of physics, which fuels my creative thinking and innovation. Specifically, I have a passion for crafting entirely novel solutions—those that have not yet been introduced to the world. This passion for groundbreaking innovation informs my approach to every project I undertake. Interestingly, these new ideas often come to me during sleep; I then strive to bring them to life in the real world. Thankfully, some of these inventions have already found practical applications in diverse fields, including the electronics industry, industrial machinery, architecture, and the aerospace industry.
