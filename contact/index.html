IMPORTANT

Please confirm the following points before contacting me:

  • If you encounter bugs or have suggestions for future improvements in my published applications and scripts, please let me know! I’m always happy to consider modifications and updates. However, when reporting bugs, please refrain from including any modifications you’ve made to the script beyond variable changes like file ID or filename. This is because I’ve only tested the published versions. For bug reports, detailed information for reproducing the issue is incredibly helpful. The more I understand how to replicate the bug, the easier it is for me to pinpoint the problem and develop a solution.

  • I’m committed to keeping my published scripts functioning optimally. If you encounter issues, it may be due to changes made by Google, library updates, or even my own oversights. In these cases, I’m dedicated to resolving the problems and updating the scripts as quickly as possible.

  • For your script and project-related issues, consider checking out community forums like Stackoverflow rather than directly contacting me. These platforms offer a wealth of knowledge and diverse perspectives from experienced users. For Google Apps Script and Google APIs-specific concerns, Google Apps Script Community and the Google Apps Script tag on Stack Overflow are excellent resources.
